Goal

Lydia wants to make some quick upgrades to her garden and doesn't know much about plants. She wants an easy way to get new plants that do well in her area.

Lydia needs to make her patio more relaxing, she wants to forget about work when she takes her breaks and doesn't have a lot of time for this change. Being busy with work she doesn't want to spend a lot of time maintaining her garden.

Behaviours and Attitudes

Lydia works from home and likes to take breaks on her patio. She works long hours and doesn't have a lot of free time. When she is not working she enjoys going out with some friends. She loves social media and is an advocate for networking on those platforms.
