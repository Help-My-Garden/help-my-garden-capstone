John Stevenson

User Story

“As a User I want to get an idea of what plants I should buy at my local plant store quickly.”

Demographics:

  • Age: 50
  • Race: White
  • Hair color: Blonde
  • Eye Color: Brown
  • Height: 6 feet
  • Job: Cashier
  • Marital status: Divorced

Technologies

  • New user
  • Desk top
  • Browser:Windows 10/Google Chrome
  • Android

Goal

John is a Cashier at the local grocery store. He recently bought a house after his divorce and wants to make some changes to his yard. He has several house plants that he really enjoys taking care of and wants to move that love for plants outside. He doesn't know much about plants but doesn't mind spending extra work on taking care of them.

Frustrations and Needs

John is not a tech savvy person so he does not want to waste time on a complicated website. John wants a fast way to get some plants for his new gardening project but does not want to spend a lot of time at a local plant store. He wants to spend less than 20 minutes at the store.

Behaviours and Attitudes

John enjoys being alone, so spending extra time taking care of plants does not bother him. John doesn't have much patience so he avoids complicated websites or apps. John joined the local garden group and wants to learn more about plants.

Interaction-flow

  • John goes to Help my Garden website
  • “Help my Garden” displays home page
  • John signs into profile
  • “Help my Garden” displays search page
  • John types in location
  • “Help my Garden” displays list local plants
  • John clicks on the Ferns
  • “Help my Garden” gives basic info on Ferns and a picture of Ferns
  • John can click add to “list”
  • “Help my Garden” places Ferns in a “list” for John
  • John can delete any Ferns that he does not want from his list
